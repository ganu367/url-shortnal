Frequently Asked Questions

What is a URL shortener?

+ -

A URL shortener is a tool that converts long URLs into shorter, more manageable links.

Why use a URL shortener?

+ -

URL shorteners are useful for sharing links on platforms with character limits, making links more user-friendly.

Use a custom URL for social media sharing?

+ -

Yes, custom URLs are commonly used for social media sharing to enhance brand visibility and provide concise links.

Benefits of customizing a URL?

+ -

Customizing URLs enhances brand visibility, increases link trustworthiness, and makes links more memorable
